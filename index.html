1. Introduction

The Socinian Exchange of Letters
A Critical Digital Edition

DFG Project, 2018-2020 (hopefully 2023)







Akademie der Wissenschaften und der Literatur | Mainz

Johannes a Lasco Bibliothek Grosse Kirche Emden

“The graph-based digital edition of Socinian correspondence, funded by the DFG, aims to provide a relevant corpus of primary sources, which, combined with analysis tools, will form a basis for conducting interdisciplinary research on the transconfessional struggle for a new balance between theology, early modern science and politics in Europe.”

Introduction:
The Socinian Letters

The Socinians

  • transnational protestant minority
  • scholarly network
  • discussion of religion, science and politics: socinianism was a precursor to the enlightenment

The Sources

  • ~2133 letters exchanged between 1580–1740
  • political reports, copperplate engravings that were enclosed in the letters
  • there is currently no complete edition
  • the originals are scattered all over Europe

GRACE

Graph Content Editor

  • Open Source - Vue.js Web Application
  • Edit and create Nodes and Relations in a neo4j Database
